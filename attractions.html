Tourist Attractions

Hallasan Mountain

Hallasan is the highest mountain in South Korea, located in the center of Jeju Island. It is a popular destination for hiking and nature enthusiasts.

Watch Video

Seongsan Ilchulbong

Seongsan Ilchulbong, also known as Sunrise Peak, is a tuff cone formed by hydrovolcanic eruptions. It is famous for its stunning sunrise views.

Watch Video

Seopjikoji

Seopjikoji is a beautiful coastal area on the east side of Jeju Island, known for its picturesque landscapes and walking trails.
